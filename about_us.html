About Us - Toby Cleaning Company Ltd

Discover Our Story

Toby Cleaning Company Limited, based in Bedfordshire, has established itself as a reputable and well-known cleaning service provider. Renowned for its swift, efficient, and honest service, the company extends its specialist domestic, commercial, and end of tenancy cleaning services across Bedfordshire​. Being a family-run venture, it prides itself on innovative cleaning solutions, effective problem-solving, and meticulous attention to detail, all with the aim of not only meeting but exceeding the expectations of every client.

Established by Metodi Ivonov (Toby), the company operates as a private limited company, with a focus on the general cleaning of buildings. The registered office is situated on Clapham Road, Bedford​.
